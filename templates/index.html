Welcome to the occupational matching system!

This is a tool that takes your resume and matches to occupations that you might be interested in and qualified for. This tool will then help you train for the appropiate job and can be useful in upgrading your job skills. Job outlook is also included to indicate if there is long-term job growth or a decline in the number of jobs in the future.

To get started, enter your resume ID and well as the number of occupations that you want to review.
